Learn more about being a data consultant

Do you love exploring diverse datasets? Want to put your skills to use, and help others achieve their goals? Gain experience consulting on real-world data problems, supporting analysis, visualization, workflow, and more.

Learn more about getting data assistance from dataCoLAB

The dataCoLAB program can help you get more from your data and create a more robust, rigorous approach to your workflow and analysis. Get help with analysis, visualization, workflow, and more.

How to write a great project description

A clear, detailed, and concise description of your research is a powerful tool to create opportunities and facilitate collaboration with others. Find out more about most important pieces of information we need.

Learn more about data
